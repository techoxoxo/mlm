01 · What is Apex

A growth game, played on a living matrix.

Apex is a strategy game where you grow a network of players and earn points as it expands. You join once, take a position in the autopool, and fill your slots as new players arrive. At every stage you make a choice: cash out your points, or climb to a bigger stage with bigger rewards.

🎯

Simple to start

One join, one small entry — then your position works for you as the matrix fills.

⚖️

Fair by design

Slots fill first-in-first-out. Whoever joined and upgraded first gets filled first — no jumping the queue.

📈

You choose the pace

Take a payout the moment a stage completes, or roll it forward and climb higher.

👑

Royalty on top

Refer players directly and share in a royalty pool paid out three times a month.

ℹ️

Important — it’s all virtual

Apex runs entirely on virtual points. There are no real-money deposits, withdrawals, or payments of any kind. Points are for play within the game.

03 · How to play

Fill your slots. Earn. Decide.

Each stage gives you a set number of slots. New players entering the autopool drop into the oldest open slot first (first-in-first-out). Every time someone fills one of your slots, their stage fee is credited to you.

When all your slots for a stage are filled, the stage is complete — and you unlock your move.

Stage 1 example

Fill 2 slots → earn each filler’s entry → stage completes → choose cash out or climb.

05 · Your move

Cash out, or climb.

Every time a stage completes you get to choose. Here’s exactly what each option gives you.

Exit

Take the payout

Cash a percentage of the points you collected at that stage and leave with it.

  • Stage 1: withdraw 100% — no restriction
  • Stages 2–4: withdraw 30%
  • Final stage: full 100% payout
Upgrade

Climb higher

The next stage’s entry fee is taken from your earnings — you keep the rest.

  • Pay the next stage’s entry fee as a seed
  • Keep everything left over instantly — no top-up needed
  • Bigger stage → more slots → larger pool

🛡️

The 5% reserve fund

5% of every royalty distribution is held back in a reserve fund. It’s shared with players who haven’t cleared a stage in the last 6 months — a safety net to keep everyone in the game. Once you receive it, you can qualify again after another 6 months under the same condition.

Note: “Direct referrals” means players who join using your link — counted separately from autopool placements. You’re always placed in your highest qualifying rank.

FAQ

How fast do my slots fill?

It depends on how many players join after you and how active your network is. The fairer FIFO queue means earlier positions fill first.

Do I have to climb?

No. You can take your payout the moment any stage completes. Climbing is optional — it just unlocks bigger pools.

What do I need for royalties?

At least 10 direct referrals to enter the Bronze rank. More directs move you up the ranks for a larger share of the pool.

Is any of this real money?

No. Apex is a virtual-points game. Points have no cash value and there are no real-money transactions.
